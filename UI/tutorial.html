Tutorial

Objective

  • Hunt the Wumpus!!!
  • Shoot the Wumpus using arrows
  • Don't die by running into the Wumpus or falling into pits

How To Play

  • Move around using large movement buttons
  • View the entire map using the minimap in the bottom right corner
    • The red space is your current location
    • Green spaces are possible moves (that may or may not contain hazards)
  • Purchase arrows and secrets using buttons on right side of screen
    • A new screen will pop up when purchasing, where you will have to answer questions
    • Answer at least 3/5 of the questions correctly to win your prize
  • View what hazards are observed nearby in the alerts field
    • Running into the Wumpus will end the game
    • Bats will pick you up and place you in a random room
    • A breeze signals a pit, which will also end the game if stepped into
